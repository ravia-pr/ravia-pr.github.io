Research Focus

Classical Cryptanalysis

Traditional attacks like differential, linear, and integral cryptanalysis applied to block ciphers.

Quantum Cryptanalysis

Grover-based and quantum SAT techniques to analyze symmetric-key primitives in the quantum model.

ML-based Cryptanalysis

Deep learning models to uncover biases, distinguishers, or keys in modern cipher structures.

Design of Symmetric Ciphers

Building efficient, secure block ciphers and AEAD schemes tailored for low-power embedded systems.
