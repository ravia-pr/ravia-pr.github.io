Research Areas

Classical Cryptanalysis

Exploring differential, linear, and algebraic techniques on modern symmetric-key primitives.

Quantum Cryptanalysis

Analyzing post-quantum security using Grover’s and Simon’s algorithms on symmetric designs.

ML-based Cryptanalysis

Utilizing machine learning models to uncover structural weaknesses and predict cryptographic behavior.

Design of Symmetric Ciphers

Contributing to the design of lightweight and secure primitives tailored for constrained environments.
