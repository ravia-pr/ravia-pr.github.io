Ravi Anand

I am an Assistant Professor in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at IIIT Delhi. I received my Ph.D. from IIT Kharagpur, where I was part of the Cryptology Research Group under the supervision of Prof. Sourav Mukhopadhyay. Following that, I completed postdoctoral research at ISI Kolkata under Prof. Subhamoy Maitra, and later at the University of Hyogo under Prof. Takanori Isobe.

My research interests lie in the cryptanalysis of symmetric ciphers ‚Äî both in classical and quantum settings. I am also involved in the design of lightweight and secure symmetric primitives. More recently, I have started exploring the application of machine learning techniques to the cryptanalysis of symmetric ciphers.

  • Symmetric Cryptography
  • Quantum Cryptanalysis
  • ML for Security
